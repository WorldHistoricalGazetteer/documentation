This section documents the staff-only tools available to WHG editors and
administrators. Access requires a Django staff account (`is_staff=True`); a
small subset of pages additionally require superuser privileges, noted
where applicable.

The WHG admin lives at the standard Django admin URL:

* Production: <https://whgazetteer.org/admin/>
* Development: <https://dev.whgazetteer.org/admin/>

Sign in with your WHG staff account to reach the admin index, from which
the pages described below are accessible.

```{note}
This section is intentionally a starting point. Several pieces — most
significantly anything related to the v3.5 Atlas UI (editorial review,
contributor workflow, retention sweep oversight, etc.) — are not yet
shipped and will be documented in detail once the v3.5 release lands.
The {doc}`./staff/atlas-v3-5` page enumerates the pieces still to come.
```
